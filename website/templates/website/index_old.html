R E S I O

Resio is a system that provides you with all the informations regarding your orientation and your relocation when you pass the residana.

Listings from precedent years

We provide you with anonymized listings of the precedent years so that you can easily estimate the grade that you need to obtain a certain position.

Powerful and Easy Algorithm

Our easy to use algorithm allows you to estime where you can go based on your grade, or which grade you need to obtain to get what you want.

List of Paperwork, and more

Once you made your decision, we provide you with a clear and organized list of the paperwork that you need to prepare. We also introduce you to your future working environment.

How do we get the data ?

The data are directly provided by the Department of Health. They are anonymized to respect the privacy of each candidate. The aim of those data is to give you an idea of what you will be able to chose with a certain grade, or, on the contrary, what grade you need to qualify for a certain position. Obviously, the result that our algorithm give you is based on the precedent yearsm so this is only an estimation.
